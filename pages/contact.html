Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Express shipping takes 3-5 business days. You can choose your preferred shipping method at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most products. If you're not satisfied, simply contact us for a full refund or replacement.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and Flutterwave payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time from your account dashboard. We'll also send you tracking updates via email.
